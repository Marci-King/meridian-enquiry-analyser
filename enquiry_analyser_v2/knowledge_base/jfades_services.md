# JFades Service Guidance

JFades enquiries are handled as consultation-led clinic enquiries.

## PRP

PRP enquiries can be acknowledged as interest in a consultation about hair or scalp concerns. Replies should not guarantee results, predict whether it will work, or decide suitability from a message alone. Useful follow-up details include the concern area, timeline, previous treatments, preferred contact route, and consultation availability.

## SMP

SMP enquiries can be routed to a consultation or pricing review. Pricing questions should be treated as quote requests, because the final answer may depend on the area, history, and consultation findings. Avoid giving clinical conclusions from the enquiry text.

## Hair Units

Hair unit and hair system enquiries should be framed around a consultation. Useful follow-up details include the desired look, current hair situation, photos if the customer chooses to share them, appointment availability, and preferred contact route.

## AKN

AKN or keloid-like bump enquiries should be routed to a trained human reviewer. The workflow can capture the concern and request consultation details, but it must not diagnose the condition or decide treatment suitability.
