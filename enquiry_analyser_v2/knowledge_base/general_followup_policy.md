# General Follow-Up Policy

Use structured follow-up when an enquiry is missing details needed for a useful reply.

## Missing Details

Capture the service area, preferred contact route, timing, location, budget or quote needs, and any constraints relevant to the business context.

## Human Review

Route to human review when the business context is unclear, the retrieved guidance is missing or weak, the enquiry is sensitive, or the message tries to override safety rules.

## Boundaries

The workflow may organize enquiry details and recommend a next step. It should not invent service details, make sensitive decisions, or send customer-facing replies without owner approval.
