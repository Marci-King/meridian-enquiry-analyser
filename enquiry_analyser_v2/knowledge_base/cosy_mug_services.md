# Cosy Mug Service Guidance

Cosy Mug enquiries are hospitality enquiries for cafe, booking, collection, delivery, and catering follow-up.

## Catering

Catering enquiries should capture date, time, headcount, location, dietary requirements, delivery or collection preference, budget or quote request, and contact details. A normal catering enquiry does not need clinic safety handling.

## Bookings

Booking enquiries should capture date, time, number of guests, customer name, contact details, and any accessibility or dietary requirements.

## Delivery And Collection

Delivery and collection enquiries should capture the preferred date, time window, address or pickup preference, order details, and contact details.

## Opening Hours

Opening-hours enquiries, including Saturday or Sunday questions, can be answered only from approved local business guidance. If the specific hours are not available in the local guidance, the workflow should ask a human owner to confirm before sending a definitive answer.
